About Me

Victoria Green

Welcome to my personal training website. There is nothing more personally & professionally satisfying that I love doing than motivating & inspiring others to reach and exceed their own expectations. Everyone possesses the tools to change their circumstances day by day… one step at a time.

Prior to launching my career as a Personal Fitness Coach, I consistently believed that staying active was an essential key to living a fruitful and fulfilling life. Throughout high school, I ran track and cross-country, played volleyball, and participated in advanced weightlifting. Through participation in these competitive sports, I discovered the strength that I needed to overcome many challenges within my life and the environment grew up in.

I hope what I am about to share may be relatable and inspiring to you. Starting in my youth throughout my teenage years, I battled bullying by classmates in school. This would ultimately lead to my experiencing and battling severe anxiety, depression and deeply rooted fears. My saving grace to cope & manage these mental and emotional challenges turned out to be my active participation in sports and fitness. In fact, I believe that doing so actually saved my life and allowed me to channel my anxiety and depression into productive and healthy gains.

During my later teenage years, I would sadly face & battle the pain and loss I felt with the passing of both my parents (each one passing in just over a year apart), facing homelessness at the age 18, enduring abusive relationships, and nearly losing my own life due to going into cardiac arrest. In all this turmoil, I became a single mother as I was blessed with two beautiful children. Then… a spark had ignited in my heart.

Enough was enough. I finally made the decision to return to the one passion and joy that I truly loved, which was and is physical fitness. Through my own fitness journey, I have not only shed pregnancy weight, but I have also healed and overcome many obstacles in my life. By channeling my pain into positive energy, I uniformly transformed my body, mind, and spirit into one of fortitude and peacefulness. And throughout this entire process & transformation, I wholeheartedly found and decided to embrace my true purpose in my life which I learned to be helping others.

I would love the opportunity to help you unlock your inner strength and enable you to discover your own greatness! Fitness allows us to unlock those attributes that make each of us unique. Everyone’s journey is their own and no one fitness plan or fitness routine applies to everyone. As a fitness coach that uses a holistic approach, I will assess any positive and negative habits/activities that may be contributing or detracting from a healthy lifestyle. We will discuss your physical fitness levels but also touch on any social, economic, financial, mental, spiritual, and/or emotional challenges that you may have. It is easy for me to empathize with each of my client’s struggles because I have equally faced many similar in nature.

As your Personal Fitness Coach, I will guide, encourage, motivate, and hold you accountable for the goals discussed during our assessment. Train with me and allow me the opportunity to guide you on your own fitness journey.
